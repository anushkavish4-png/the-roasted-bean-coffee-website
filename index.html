Our Story

Founded in 2015, The Roasted Bean began as a simple dream: to serve the perfect cup of coffee in a space that feels like home.

We source our beans from sustainable farms in Ethiopia and Colombia, roasting them locally to ensure every sip is fresh. Whether you need a quiet corner to work or a lively space to catch up with friends, our doors are always open.
